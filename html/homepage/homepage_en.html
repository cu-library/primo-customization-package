Welcome to Omni!

Omni is Carleton's new, easy-to-use search tool that gives you access to high-quality academic resources.

  • Powerful search tool for discovery and in-depth research
  • Quick and streamlined access to resources
  • Intuitive mobile-friendly user experience

Sign in to access your library account, renew loans, and more!

 Where can I get help?
  • Our OMNI Help Guide provides search tips and help on finding electronic resources with OMNI.
  • Faculty and instructors can get help with online teaching and research.
  • Get support by submitting one of our forms to report electronic resource connection problems, account assistance, and other inquiries.
See COVID-19 Updates and Changes to Library Services for information about accessing the library and our services.

All digital and electronic materials are still accessible with OMNI and research support is still available online. Some physical format materials can be borrowed through our Curbside Pick-Up and Mail Delivery Service or through HathiTrust Emergency Temporary Access Service.
